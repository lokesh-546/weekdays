Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 23, 2026

Welcome to GoNestro. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how GoNestro collects, uses, shares, and protects your data when you use our platform, including our website, mobile application, and services.

By using GoNestro, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.

We collect information to provide better services and improve your experience.

a) Personal Information

b) Property & Transaction Information

  • Property details you list or search
  • Preferences, interests, and interactions

c) Technical Information

2. How We Use Your Information

GoNestro uses your information to:

  • Enhance platform security and prevent fraud

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal data. However, we may share information in the following cases:

  • With other users (e.g., sharing your contact details when you inquire or list a property)

We take appropriate measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, misuse, or loss. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights & Choices

You have the right to access and update your personal information, request deletion of your data, opt-out of marketing communications, and control notification preferences.

GoNestro is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
