Effective Date: April 23, 2026

Refund Policy

At GoNestro, we strive to provide high-quality real estate services and a smooth user experience. This Refund Policy explains the conditions under which refunds may be granted for payments made on our platform.

By using GoNestro and making payments, you agree to this Refund Policy.

1. General Policy

  • Payments made for premium listings, promotions, subscriptions, or service fees are generally non-refundable.
  • Refunds are only considered in specific cases outlined below.
  • All refund requests are subject to review and approval by GoNestro.

2. Eligible Refund Scenarios

You may be eligible for a refund if:

  • A technical error resulted in duplicate or failed transactions.
  • You were charged incorrectly due to a system issue.
  • The paid service was not delivered as promised due to a fault from GoNestro.
  • Payment was made but the service was not activated within a reasonable time.

3. Non-Refundable Cases

Refunds will not be provided in the following situations:

  • Change of mind after purchasing a service.
  • Lack of results (e.g., not receiving leads or inquiries).
  • User error in selecting services or plans.
  • Delays caused by third-party service providers.
  • Removal or suspension of listings due to policy violations.

4. Refund Request Process

To request a refund, please follow these steps:

  • Email us at support@gonestro.com
  • Include your registered details, transaction ID, and reason for request.
  • Submit your request within 7 days of the transaction.

Our team will review and respond within 5–10 business days.

5. Refund Processing

  • Approved refunds will be processed to the original payment method.
  • Processing time may take 7–15 business days, depending on your bank or payment provider.

Cancellation Policy

This Cancellation Policy outlines how users can cancel services or subscriptions on GoNestro.

1. Service Cancellation

Users may cancel certain services based on the type of service purchased.

a) Listings & Promotions

  • Once a property listing or promotion is activated, it cannot be cancelled.
  • Users may choose to remove or deactivate listings, but no refund will be issued.

b) Subscription Plans (if applicable)

  • Users can cancel subscriptions anytime.
  • Cancellation will stop future billing, but current plan benefits will continue until expiry.
  • No partial refunds for unused duration.

2. Cancellation by GoNestro

GoNestro reserves the right to cancel or suspend services if:

  • Users violate Terms & Conditions.
  • Fraudulent or misleading activities are detected.
  • Required information or verification is not provided.

No refund will be issued in such cases.

3. Third-Party Services Cancellation

  • Cancellation policies for services provided by third parties (legal, construction, etc.) may vary.
  • GoNestro is not responsible for third-party cancellation terms or refunds.
  • Users are advised to review third-party policies before booking.
